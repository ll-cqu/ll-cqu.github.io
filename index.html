Biography
I am currently a postgraduate student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHKSZ) under the esteemed guidance of Prof. Baoyuan Wu. I am also honored to be in active cooperation with Prof. Siwei Lyu (BU), Dr. Yong Zhang (Tencent AILab), and Taiping Yao (Tencent YoutuLab). My research primarily focuses on AIGC/Face Forgery/Deepfake detection and AI4Science. I am now an incoming PhD student at Peking University, supervised by Prof. Li Yuan.
